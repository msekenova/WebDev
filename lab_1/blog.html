12 things that help to improve your productivity

1. What is productivity
Time, energy, and attention are what makes productivity.
2. The rule of three
Before starting work, identify 3 main tasks for yourself - this is the golden mean. Then break them down into small sub-tasks and get to work.
3. What to do if you don't want to do anything - Make a list of negative consequences due to a case not done.
4. Social networks? What is this?
Take time to respond in messengers.
5. Off notice
Turn off notifications to immerse yourself in work with your head and complete tasks much faster.
6. The path to productivity through exercise - increases productivity and concentration.
7. Ask yourself for advice
If a problem arises and you need advice, ask yourself first!
8. Productivity and happiness Investing time in productivity can mean that you are not satisfied with the current state of affairs.

4 great ways to deal with insomnia

1. It is necessary to go to bed on time so that neurotransmitters in the brain are produced correctly and fully. Approximately at 22: 00-23: 00. It is very important.
2.️ Experts advise to sleep in complete darkness, coolness and silence.
3.️ Before going to bed, it is advisable to take a walk in the fresh air.
4.️ Meditative and relaxing practices and calm music are also helpful.
